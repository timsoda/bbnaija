About

WETIN DE SUP FOR HERE?

This platform was created for those that like Shalaye.
With this platform we could be able to predict the house mates that have more votes, since bbnaija2020 voting platform does not show us the voting result until after the eviction or the show. This platform will actually be showing you votes in real-time once you vote.

WHY DO I NEED EMAIL TO VOTE?

To limit alot of random vote, your votes are locked to your email.
However you need to provide your email only once and a code will be sent to your email.
With this code you can be able to vote ten(10) times daily till Thursday midnight.
Your email won't be revealed to anyone and We promise not to spam you.
You can opt-out of this platform at anytime.
An email will only be sent once to you when you register.

WHY TEN TIMES A DAY VOTE?

As we already know, the ORIGINAL bbnaija 2020 platform allows 100 votes per sim card for a week, but this platform will accept ten(10) vote from each email per day, and the voting closes every Thursday midnight.
